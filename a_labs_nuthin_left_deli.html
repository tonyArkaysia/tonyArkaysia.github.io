A Owner and Staff - Back of House

Operational control for a grocery + hot food hybrid. Built to keep specials fresh, guard against fake orders, and keep the kitchen moving.

Context: Bodega + hot food line + rotating South American lunch specials in a school-heavy, high-foot-traffic residential + commercial zone in Elmhurst. Already uses Square, but needs smarter ops + marketing + student-friendly ordering.

Product and Menu Catalog

Grocery + prepared foods
  • Master list with name, category, tags (school snack, lunch special), price, cost, available-today flag.
  • Everyday menu plus daily specials by weekday.
  • Tags power student-facing filters.

Daily Specials and Schedule

Images, limits, templates
  • Set today's specials with photos and blurbs; toggle limited quantity (e.g., 25 plates).
  • Weekly templates (Mon-Fri school schedule, Sat/Sun alternate).
  • Pushes to student app and marketing blasts automatically.

Order Board (Pickup + Delivery)

Status-aware workflow
  • New -> In Kitchen -> Ready -> Picked Up / Out for Delivery -> Completed.
  • Pickup for students/neighbors; delivery for local area.
  • Square link/QR for payment; unpaid orders cannot enter the kitchen.

Delivery Rules and Guardrails

Reduce abuse and no-shows
  • Prepaid-only delivery option; minimum order and optional delivery fee.
  • Track phone/email; flag repeat cancellers for prepaid-only.
  • Limit orders from risky profiles unless prepaid.

Inventory Lite

Signals, not heavy ERP
  • Track key grocery SKUs and hot food portions.
  • Low-stock indicators and fast counts on what kids actually buy.
  • Ties to specials (e.g., chicken plate x 25).

Owner Dashboard

What matters today
  • Orders by pickup vs. delivery.
  • Hot specials sold and top school-time items (11am-4pm).
  • Repeat customers from nearby schools.

B Student and Customer - Front of House

Mobile-first, no heavy accounts. Kids see what is hot right now, pay fast via Square, and pick a pickup time for the lunch rush.

"Today at Nuthin' Left Deli" Home

  • Today's Hot Specials with big photos and simple labels.
  • Quick Bites (chips, snacks, drinks) and Student Combos (meal + drink bundles).
  • School-friendly categories: Before School, Lunch Period, After School Snacks.

Simple Flow

  • Pick items -> choose pickup/delivery -> add name + school (optional) + phone.
  • Square checkout (card/Apple Pay/Google Pay); pre-schedule pickup windows like 12:30, 1:00, 3:00.
  • Time-window-only tags: "Beat the Bell Deals" for 20-30 minute blocks.

C Marketing Engine for a Bodega Next to Schools

Own the student list, send hyper-local deals, and make every special visible in-app and over SMS/email.

Student Signup Flows

  • QR codes on windows, counter, and near bus stops.
  • Landing: "Join the Nuthin' Left Deals list" -> name + phone/email.
  • Instantly see today's specials; prompt to save to home screen as a pseudo-app.

Segmented Broadcasts

  • Blasts like "Lomo Saltado $8.99 until 2:30pm" or "Empanada + drink $5, 3-5pm."
  • Segments: Students (school-time) and Neighborhood (evening/weekend).
  • Channels: SMS (with limits), email, optional WhatsApp/link broadcast.

Limited-Time and Quantity Deals

  • Create a deal with items, time window, optional quantity limit.
  • Displays on student app, pushes to chosen segments, shows countdown.

Admin and Roles

  • Owner: full access. Staff: order screen + ready/done only.
  • Protects pricing and marketing tools from the rush counter.

D Analytics That Answer Real Questions

Show what actually moves between 11am and 4pm, which specials land, and where to tighten offers.

Student Heat

  • Top 10 student items by time window.
  • Pickup vs. delivery ratio.

Specials Performance

  • Sales per special by day.
  • Which deals get ignored so you can replace them.

Customer Cohorts

  • New vs. returning students.
  • Repeat customers by school.
