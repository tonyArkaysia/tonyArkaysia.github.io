🧬 Cannabis Science

Dive into the scientific breakdown of cannabinoids, terpenes, and how they influence your experience.

🌱 Cannabinoids

Cannabinoids are active compounds in cannabis that interact with the body’s endocannabinoid system.

Cannabinoid Effects Common Strains
THC (Δ9-Tetrahydrocannabinol) Psychoactive, euphoria, pain relief White Widow, Apple Jacks
CBD (Cannabidiol) Anti-inflammatory, relaxation, anxiety relief Afghani, Sunday Driver
CBG (Cannabigerol) Neuroprotective, anti-inflammatory Wild Thai, Lemon Cherry Pie

🍊 Terpenes

Terpenes are aromatic compounds responsible for cannabis flavors and effects.

🌿 Myrcene

Relaxation, sedation, earthy aroma.

Common Strains: Afghani, Maraschino Runtz

🍋 Limonene

Mood elevation, citrus aroma.

Common Strains: Wild Thai, Confucius

🌶️ Caryophyllene

Pain relief, anti-inflammatory.

Common Strains: White Widow, Major-Z

🧪 How It Works

The cannabinoids and terpenes in cannabis interact with receptors in the brain and body to produce various effects.
