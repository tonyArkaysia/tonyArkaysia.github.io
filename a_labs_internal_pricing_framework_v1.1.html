üìê Complexity Points (CP) System

CP is our internal way to measure how ‚Äúbig‚Äù a build really is. It prevents random pricing and keeps barbers, bodegas, and restaurants all quoted consistently.

üìÜ Project Stages & Payment Structure

This structure is both industry-standard and easy to explain. We keep it the same across projects unless there‚Äôs a strong reason to deviate.

  • Stage 1 ‚Äì Kickoff & Core Setup (33%)
    Scope locking, workflow mapping, base project setup, DB schema, initial screens.
  • Stage 2 ‚Äì Core Build (33%)
    Primary flows live in staging (ordering, dashboard, core UX, integrations wired).
  • Stage 3 ‚Äì Refinement & Launch (34%)
    Polish, marketing hooks, analytics, training, and go-live support.

For all stages: payment is due when we deliver the agreed milestone to staging. If a client cancels, we stop at the last paid stage; work completed remains ours until paid.
