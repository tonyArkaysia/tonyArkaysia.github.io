Our Process

Iterate to impact: observe → prototype → measure → refine → deploy.

1) Discovery

Problem framing with the people who live it. Define success metrics and constraints up front.

2) Prototype

Rapid CAD + 3D prints. Software MVPs that exercise the core workflow without bloat.

3) Field Test

Deploy in real settings. Capture feedback, reliability, safety, and measurable outcomes.

4) Scale

Harden materials, refine UX, document, and replicate the solution where it’s needed.
