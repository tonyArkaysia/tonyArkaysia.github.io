Bernard King: A Basketball Legend

Published on December 4, 2023, at 10:30 AM (UTC)

Author: ISL Atlas

Bernard King, a legendary small forward in the NBA, strategically crafted his scoring approach and utilized precise positioning throughout his illustrious career. His insights into the game, as mentioned in the, played a crucial role in his remarkable achievements.

During his prime years, spanning 14 seasons with six seasons at the peak of his career with the Golden State Warriors (GSW) and the New York Knicks (NYK), King showcased extraordinary scoring efficiency. His field goal percentages are a testament to his prowess: with GSW, he boasted an impressive .577 FG%, and with NYK, he maintained a remarkable .543 FG%.

What sets King apart is his standing among the all-time greats in terms of field goal percentage. Currently, he holds the 6th position in the highest career field goal percentage, an outstanding achievement given that this ranking includes players with over 2000 field goals made. This is particularly notable considering King's position as a 6'7" small forward, highlighting his exceptional scoring ability from various spots on the floor.

King's scoring prowess extended beyond mere percentages; he was a prolific scorer known for his dominance in an era filled with defensive stalwarts. His ability to deliver monster scoring performances was a hallmark of his career. Notably, even late into the 80s and early 90s, King recorded multiple 50-point games, showcasing his scoring consistency and offensive mastery.

One of the standout moments in King's career is his high-scoring game, where he dropped an impressive 60 points. This accomplishment not only solidified his status as one of the premier scorers in the league but also underscored his resilience and skill, as he continued to amass incredible point totals well into the latter stages of his playing days.

Bernard King's scoring efficiency, strategic approach, and remarkable achievements on the court have left an indelible mark on the history of the NBA, making him an enduring figure in the realm of basketball greatness.
